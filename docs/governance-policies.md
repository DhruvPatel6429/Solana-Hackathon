# Governance Policies

## Policy Categories
- `PAYOUT_APPROVAL_LIMITS`
- `TREASURY_DUAL_CONTROL`
- `WEBHOOK_SECURITY`
- `API_KEY_LIFECYCLE`
- `RECOVERY_DRILL_CADENCE`

## Storage
Policies are stored in `GovernancePolicy` with organization/company scope and audit metadata.

## Change Management
- Policy updates must be initiated by organization admins.
- Changes are logged in audit records.
- Emergency overrides must be documented with incident references.
